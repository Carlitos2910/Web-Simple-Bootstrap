ARK II

ARK 2 es la secuela de ARK: Survival Evolved. Este juego fue anunciado en el Game Awards 2020, y se tratará de un juego tipo sandbox de supervivencia, de forma similar al primero. El juego se lanzará en consolas de nueva generación.

Maps

A map is an spawn area, where the survivor start his adventure with a random probability of appear in each part of the map.

Events

Events are special occasions on ARK: Survival Evolved that can consist of boosted rates, limited-time content, or permanent content.

Dinos

Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles and one type of many creatures to be found in ARK: Survival Evolved. This list includes only non-avian dinosaurs, not Birds.
